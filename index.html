About

Hey! My name's Bernardo Arevalo. I am graduating UCSD student majoring in Bioengineering: Bioinformatics.


Over the course of my major I have centered my programming skills to help people with their health. My inspiration for this stems from the fact that I have lived now with Diabetes for six years of my life. I feel comfortable and excited learning new technologies and developing web apps to better the management of day to day health. Whether the project consists of finding new ways to deal with patients' documents or glucose readings of a diabetic, it brings personal happiness knowing they can all better the health of others.
